Maps & Direction

Located within 1KM of the Seomyeon intersection, the heart of Busan, ON Hospital offers convenient one-stop services for patients in the Seomyeon area.
Unlike other hospitals, it can be easily accessed through non-stop buses and subway stations, making it highly accessible.
