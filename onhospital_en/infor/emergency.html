Emergency Center

Emergency Medical Treatment Procedure

Move from side to side.
01. Application for Treatment
If you fill out a treatment application form at the emergency payment counter and submit it, a medical admission receipt will be issued.
02. Triage Room (Initial Screening Room)
When you go to the triage room, you will be assigned to a medical area based on the severity of your condition after a preliminary examination.
03. Examination
Submit the medical admission receipt to the emergency room nurse in the assigned medical area and the attending physician will provide emergency medical treatment.
The decision to discharge or admit the patient will be made after the treatment.

※ Medical Departments with Emergency Room Specialists on Duty
Internal Medicine, Pediatrics, Psychiatry, Neurology, Dermatology, Surgery, Orthopedics, Neurosurgery, Thoracic Surgery, Plastic Surgery, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Ophthalmology, Otorhinolaryngology, Urology, Rehabilitation Medicine, Family Medicine, Dentistry, Anesthesiology and Pain Medicine, Radiology, Imaging Medicine, Korean Medicine, Diagnostic Laboratory Medicine
04. Payment of Medical Expenses
Patients who receive a decision to discharge should pay their medical expenses at the emergency payment counter and receive a receipt and an outpatient clinic reservation slip.
05. Medication
Upon discharge, consult with medical staff about the following treatment information and precautions when returning home.
For prescriptions that can be filled outside of the hospital, present the prescription at a nearby pharmacy to purchase the medication.
06. Admission
Patients who receive a decision to admit should proceed with admission procedures at the admission counter.
