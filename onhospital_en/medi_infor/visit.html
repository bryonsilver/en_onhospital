Visiting a Patient

General ward

Weekdays 6pm to 8pm
Weekends and public holidays 10am to 12pm / 6pm to 8pm
※ In order to ensure the stability and rapid recovery of hospitalized patients and prevent infection, we limit visiting hours. We kindly request visitors to cooperate with us despite any inconvenience.

Intensive care unit

Lunch 12:30pm to 1pm
Dinner 6:30pm to 7pm

Guidelines for visitors

※ Children under 6 years old and the elderly with weak immune systems are prohibited from visiting the hospital due to the risk of contracting infectious diseases.
※ Visits are restricted for patients who are hospitalized with infectious diseases.
